About

I'm Denis, a self-taught builder from Bosnia. I make AI systems that talk to real customers and move real money, not demos that only look good in a screenshot.

I work solo and end to end. I design it, write the code, set up the servers, and stay on it until the thing actually works in the wild. So far that has meant voice agents that call and close, software that reads the ad market, and automation that runs for weeks without anyone touching it.

One question matters to me more than any other: does it still work when nobody is watching. Most of what I build is answering a call or sending an email right now, without me in the room.
